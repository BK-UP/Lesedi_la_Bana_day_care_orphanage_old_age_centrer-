About Us

We as Lesedi la bana day care orphanage and old age centre we focus on providing care, food, education, and other necessities for children with disadvantaged background. Furthermore we also provide elderly day care center whereby elders can engage in fun outdoor and indoor activities. We have created a platform where all aged groups from children, youth and elderly people socially engage in positivity and good faith. Our aim is to keep children off the streets by positively impacting their lives and also to keep the elderly active as possible with age. We pride ourselves in serving our community and changing lives daily.

Values and Ethics

  • Professionalism in all our services
  • Serve with compassion
  • High level of good morals and integrity
  • Build mutual relationships based on trust, quality and commitment
  • Add value to our community
